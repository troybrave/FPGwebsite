§ 07
New here?

We were hoping
you'd come.

Walking in cold to a new church is hard. Here's exactly what to expect — and a way to say hi before you do.

No pressure.
No performance.
Just come.

Wear what you wore on Saturday. Bring the kids. Sit in the back if you want. We'll find you if you'd like us to, and leave you alone if you wouldn't.

01
Park in the blue spaces.
First-time visitor parking. Easy walk to the front door — a real person will be there to say good morning.
02
Stop by the welcome desk.
Grab a coffee, get a gift for showing up, and we'll walk you to the kids' area if you need to drop them off.
03
Find a seat — any seat.
Worship is about 25 minutes. Preaching is about 40. We wrap by 11:15 and nobody's in a rush to leave.
04
Come back next week.
Or don't. No follow-up call, no pressure. But we'd love to see you again, and we mean it.
